Who I Am

I am Engr.Md.Oleullah (Oli) — an Electrical & Electronic Engineer, Cyber Security professional, Web Developer, Researcher, and serial Entrepreneur based in Bangladesh.

My career spans multiple high-impact domains: from designing electronic systems and securing digital infrastructures to building healthcare and media enterprises. As the Managing Director of Health Guard Bangladesh Ltd., I lead initiatives that bridge technology and public health.

As the Founder of an Electronics Business and Publisher of Khabor Bangla, I combine engineering precision with entrepreneurial vision. My academic work has resulted in 3 published research papers, and I hold a Government-certified Pharmacy Technician credential (Category-C).

I believe the most meaningful problems sit at the intersection of engineering, security, and business — and I have dedicated my career to solving them.
